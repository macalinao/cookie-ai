Your hands-free cooking companion.

Cookie makes it easy to cook food without having to manage cookbooks. Using the Amazon Alexa and an intuitive natural language interface, Cookie converses with you as if she were a chef teaching you what to do.

Cook anything.

Using the powerful BigOven API, Cookie allows you to choose from a myriad of dishes. Always leave your family or guests excited with new recipes every day.

Seamlessly integrated.

Cookie integrates well with Alexa, not interrupting its normal features but allowing you to continue to use Alexa's powerful capabilities as you cook your creations. Features such as unit conversion and definition lookup are not inhibited while using Cookie. Most importantly, you can cut your vegetables to the beat of your favorite musicians!

Built in 24 hours at Greylock Hackfest 2015 by Ian Macalinao, Soon Chee Loong, Andrew Feng, and Pradeep Karuturi.
